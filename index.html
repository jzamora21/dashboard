The Top Ten places to visit in the United States of America

Rank #1

Glacier National Park

Montana

Glacier National Park is one of the most visited places in the US. It has two mountain ranges and includes more than 700 lakes across the state of Montana. Glacier is known for its vertical mountains, lakes, forests and streams. Many people come to visit the park for its famous section "Going-to-the-sun-road" that was constructed during the great depression.
Rank #2

Yellowstone National Park

Wyoming


Yellowstone National Park has a two million acre that stretches across Wyoming, Montana, and Idaho that is special in its nature. It features many dramatic canyon, rivers, and more. It is home to many animal species such as bears, wolves, elk and more. The best times to visit the park are from late April to May as well as September through early October.
Rank #1

Siesta Key Beach

Florida

Siesta Key is an eight mile long island where you can go and get a relaxing gateway in Florida's South westcoast. You can take the free trolly service and get around the island. Many visitors love to experience the powdery sand and the soft waves and the abundance of sun & fun at Siesta Beach.
Rank #2

Maui Beach

Hawaii

Maui extends to thirty miles of beaches. You'll find white or black sand beaches. There is a lot of spots to surf or simply swim, snorkel or sunbathe. The best time to visit is from April to May or from September to November.
Rank #1

Statue Of Liberty

New York

The Statue Of Liberty is one of the most recognizable figures in the world. The liberty island is located in the New York Harbor and can only be reached by ferry boat. The best time to visit the Statue Of Liberty is from Tuesday-Thursday during spring or fall.
Rank #2

The White House

Washington DC

The White House has been the seat of the US goverment and home of the Presidents of the United States of America for more than 200 years. Tourists are encouraged to schedule a tour as early as possible because many spots fill up quickly.
Rank #1

The Metropolitan Museum of Art

New York

This museum is located in Manhattan, New York. This is considered to be the largest museum in the US and fourth largest museum in the world. It contains more than 1.5 million pieces of work. Its collection spans 5,000 years of world culture, from prehistory to the present and from every part of the globe.
Rank #2

Smithsonian National Museum of Natural History

Washington Dc

The Smithnosian National Museum of Natural History is located in Washington, Dc. This museum connects to people to the unfold history of the Earth. It is free admission and is open to the public everyday. The Smithnosian museum han 600 specimens of precious stones, including meteorites, diamonds, emeralds, rubies, sapphires, and other treasures.
Rank #1

Grand Canyon National Park

Arizona

The Grand Canyon is located in Northern Arizona and it is one of the greatest tourist attractions in the United States. It's colorful landscape and scenery attracts people from around the world. The best time to visit is from April to May where the weather is smooth for visitors.
Rank #2

Manhattan

New York

Manhattan is one of the New York's five boroughs. It's skyline is the most appealing to many tourists and there are so many things to do such as catch a broadway show, climb the Empire State Building and more. You can also visit times square where the famous ball drop happens every new year.
